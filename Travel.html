My Travel Blog

This blog describes the tourist places I have visited in chronological order along with my personal experiences.

1. Hyderabad

Hyderabad was the first major city I visited. It is known for its rich history, delicious food, and beautiful monuments. Visiting Charminar and Golconda Fort was a memorable experience for me.

  • Birla temple
  • Charminar

2. Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram is a coastal city famous for its culture and beaches. I visited Marina Beach and enjoyed the sunrise. The city has a unique blend of tradition and modern life.
